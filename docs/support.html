Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?
BluTask supports Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google. Tap either button on the login screen — no password required.
My tasks aren't syncing across devices. What should I do?
Make sure you're signed in to the same account on both devices and have an active internet connection. Pull down to refresh the task list. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in.
How do I enable push notifications?
Go to the Settings tab inside BluTask and tap Enable push notifications. If you previously denied permission, go to iOS Settings → BluTask → Notifications and enable them there.
How do I use Quick Import (AI feature)?
On the School tab, tap the import icon and paste any text — a syllabus, assignment list, or email. The AI will detect and parse the assignments for you to review and add.
How do I delete a task or assignment?
Swipe left on any task or assignment in the list to reveal the delete option, or tap the item to open it and use the delete button.
Can I delete my account and all my data?
Yes. Email us at samerbaderwork@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we will permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
BluTask isn't loading or shows a blank screen.
Force-close the app and reopen it. If the issue continues, delete and reinstall BluTask from the App Store. Your data is synced to the cloud and will be restored after signing back in.
