Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 11, 2026

BluTask ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal productivity app for managing tasks, school assignments, and schedules. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights over your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address and display name, provided when you sign in with Apple or Google.
  • App content — tasks, assignments, classes, notes, and due dates you create inside the app.
  • Device token — a push notification token if you grant notification permission, used only to send you reminders you configure.

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or any data unrelated to the app's core function.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Sync your tasks and assignments across devices.
  • Send push notification reminders you set up yourself.
  • Authenticate you securely via Apple or Google.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Third-Party Services

BluTask uses the following services to operate:

  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider. Your data is stored on Supabase's servers. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Apple — Apple's authentication service. See Apple Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Google — Google's authentication service. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) — used to deliver reminders to your device.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at samerbaderwork@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of the request.

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with encryption at rest via Supabase. We follow industry-standard practices to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

6. Children's Privacy

BluTask is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for notifications at any time via iOS Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at samerbaderwork@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
