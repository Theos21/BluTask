## Description (4000 chars max)
BluTask is the productivity app built for students and busy professionals who need everything in one place. Stop juggling separate apps for your to-do list, homework tracker, and calendar — BluTask brings them together in a clean, focused interface designed to keep you on top of what matters.

**Smart Task Management**
Create, prioritize, and organize tasks with due dates, priorities, and labels. Drag to reorder, swipe to complete, and use the built-in archive to keep your workspace tidy. The command palette lets power users navigate and create tasks in seconds without lifting their hands from the keyboard.

**School Assignment Tracking**
Dedicated school mode lets you track assignments by subject, set deadlines, and monitor your academic workload separately from personal tasks. Never miss a submission again — BluTask keeps your coursework front and center.

**Calendar Integration**
The built-in calendar gives you a full monthly and weekly view of your tasks and events. See everything at a glance and plan your week with confidence. Upcoming deadlines surface automatically so you're never caught off guard.

**Built for Focus**
BluTask features a refined dark interface that reduces eye strain during late-night study sessions. Everything is fast, responsive, and distraction-free — no ads, no noise.

**Sync Across Devices**
Your data syncs instantly via Supabase so your tasks, assignments, and calendar events are always up to date whether you're on your iPhone or the web.

Download BluTask and take control of your day.
